Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small gathering of developers and innovators. Since then, it has grown into one of the world’s leading technology conferences, attracting thousands of participants each year. From its humble beginnings, TechCon has expanded across multiple continents, driving collaboration and breakthrough innovation in the tech industry.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to connect people, ideas, and technology to shape a better future. By providing a platform for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and inspiration, TechCon empowers professionals and enthusiasts to make meaningful contributions to the tech ecosystem.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed some of the most influential voices in the technology world. Here are a few of our distinguished past speakers:

Ada Lovelace – Widely regarded as the first computer programmer, Ada inspired attendees with her vision of computational possibilities.

Alan Turing – A pioneer of artificial intelligence and computer science, Turing’s legacy continues to influence modern computing.

Grace Hopper – Known as the ‘mother of COBOL’, Grace Hopper inspired audiences with her work in computer programming and software development.
